Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our treatments, procedures, and services

What makes Advanced Aesthetics different from other medical spas?

+

Advanced Aesthetics combines medical expertise with luxury spa experiences. Our board-certified medical professionals use the latest FDA-approved technologies and medical-grade treatments to deliver safe, effective results. We focus on personalized care plans tailored to each client's unique needs and goals.

Are your treatments safe?

+

Yes, all our treatments are performed by licensed medical professionals using FDA-approved equipment and techniques. We conduct thorough consultations and medical assessments before any procedure to ensure your safety and optimal results.

Do I need a consultation before treatment?

+

Yes, we require a consultation for all new clients and most treatments. This allows us to assess your skin, discuss your goals, review your medical history, and create a personalized treatment plan that's right for you.

What should I expect during my first visit?

+

Your first visit includes a comprehensive consultation where we'll discuss your concerns, goals, and medical history. We'll perform a skin analysis, explain treatment options, and may take before photos. If you're ready to proceed, some treatments can be performed the same day.

What age groups do you treat?

+

We treat clients 18 years and older. Our treatments range from preventative care for younger clients to corrective treatments for mature skin. We customize our approach based on your age, skin type, and aesthetic goals.

How long do Botox results last?

+

Botox results typically last 3-4 months. Results may vary based on individual factors such as metabolism, muscle strength, and lifestyle. Regular maintenance treatments help maintain optimal results.

Are dermal fillers painful?

+

Most clients experience minimal discomfort. We use topical numbing cream and many of our fillers contain lidocaine for added comfort. Most describe the sensation as a slight pinch or pressure.

How many laser treatments will I need?

+

The number of treatments varies depending on your specific concerns and the type of laser treatment. Most clients see optimal results with 3-6 sessions spaced 4-6 weeks apart. We'll create a personalized treatment plan during your consultation.

Can I combine different treatments?

+

Yes, many treatments can be safely combined for enhanced results. Our medical professionals will recommend the best combination approach based on your goals and skin condition. Some treatments may need to be spaced apart for optimal healing.

When will I see results from my treatment?

+

Results vary by treatment type. Botox shows results in 3-7 days, fillers are immediate with final results in 2 weeks, and laser treatments show gradual improvement over several weeks to months. We'll discuss expected timelines during your consultation.

How do I book an appointment?

+

You can book online through our website, call us directly, or use our mobile app. We recommend booking consultations at least 1-2 weeks in advance, especially for first-time visits.

What is your cancellation policy?

+

We require 24-hour notice for cancellations or rescheduling. Late cancellations or no-shows may be subject to a fee. We understand emergencies happen and will work with you when possible.

Do you offer payment plans?

+

Yes, we offer flexible payment options including CareCredit financing and treatment packages. We also have membership programs that provide discounts on regular treatments and products.

What forms of payment do you accept?

+

We accept cash, credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), CareCredit, and HSA/FSA cards for qualifying treatments. Payment is due at the time of service unless other arrangements have been made.

Do you offer package deals or discounts?

+

Yes, we offer treatment packages, seasonal promotions, and loyalty rewards. Our membership program provides ongoing discounts and exclusive offers. Follow us on social media or join our newsletter for the latest deals.
