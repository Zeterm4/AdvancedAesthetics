Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our laser treatments

Are laser treatments safe?

+

Yes, our laser treatments are FDA-approved and performed by trained medical professionals. We use the latest technology with built-in safety features and conduct thorough consultations to ensure treatments are appropriate for your skin type.

How many sessions will I need?

+

The number of sessions varies depending on the treatment type, area being treated, and individual factors. Hair removal typically requires 6-8 sessions, while skin resurfacing may need 3-5 treatments. We'll provide a personalized treatment plan during your consultation.

What should I expect during treatment?

+

Most patients describe laser treatments as feeling like a rubber band snapping against the skin. We use cooling systems and topical anesthetics when needed to maximize comfort. Treatment times vary from 15 minutes to 90 minutes depending on the area and procedure.

Is there any downtime after laser treatments?

+

Downtime varies by treatment type. Hair removal typically has no downtime, while resurfacing treatments may require 3-7 days of healing. We'll provide detailed aftercare instructions and schedule follow-up appointments as needed.

When will I see results?

+

Results timeline varies by treatment. Hair removal shows gradual reduction over several sessions, skin resurfacing improvements appear within 1-2 weeks, and tattoo removal shows fading after each session. Full results develop over the complete treatment series.

How do I prepare for my laser treatment?

+

Preparation instructions vary by treatment but generally include avoiding sun exposure, discontinuing certain skincare products, and following specific pre-treatment guidelines. We'll provide detailed preparation instructions during your consultation.
