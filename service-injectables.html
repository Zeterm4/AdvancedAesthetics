Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about injectable treatments

How long do injectable treatments last?

+

Results vary by treatment type. Botox typically lasts 3-4 months, while dermal fillers can last 6-18 months depending on the product used and treatment area. Our team will discuss expected longevity during your consultation.

Are injectable treatments painful?

+

Most clients experience minimal discomfort. We use topical numbing cream and ice to maximize comfort. Many of our fillers also contain lidocaine for additional pain relief during treatment.

What is the recovery time?

+

Injectable treatments require minimal downtime. You may experience slight swelling or bruising for 1-3 days. Most clients return to normal activities immediately after treatment.

When will I see results?

+

Dermal filler results are visible immediately, with final results after swelling subsides (1-2 weeks). Botox results begin to appear in 3-5 days, with full effects visible in 10-14 days.

Are there any side effects?

+

Side effects are typically mild and temporary, including slight swelling, bruising, or redness at injection sites. Serious complications are rare when treatments are performed by qualified professionals.

How much do injectable treatments cost?

+

Costs vary based on treatment type and amount needed. Botox starts at $12/unit, fillers start at $650/syringe. We offer complimentary consultations to provide accurate pricing based on your goals.
